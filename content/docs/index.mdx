---



**Ech0** is a new-generation open-source self-hosted platform designed for individual users.
It is ultra-lightweight and low-cost, supporting the ActivityPub protocol to let you easily publish and share ideas, writings, and links. With a clean, intuitive interface and powerful command-line tools, content management becomes simple and flexible.
Your data is fully owned and controlled by you, always connected to the world, building your own network of thoughts.
